How To Play

Drag dominos onto the board.

Tap a domino to rotate it.

Match 3+ same tiles to merge them into the next value.

Chain reactions earn bonus points!

Red 6s explode and vanish when matched.

Chaos mode: matches of 4+ tiles collapse the whole board, letting tiles fall to fill the gaps — this can cascade into more combos!

Ultra Chaos: same as Chaos, but once the board starts collapsing, any match of 3+ keeps it collapsing again and again until nothing matches anymore.

Game ends when no moves remain.
